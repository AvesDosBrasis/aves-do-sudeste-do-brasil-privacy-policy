# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Aves do Sudeste do Brasil is designed to work offline. This version does not
require account creation, does not request precise location, does not record
audio from the user, and does not depend on a proprietary backend for the main
guide experience to work in trails or areas without service.

The app stores only local data needed for normal use, including guide layout
mode, sort mode, selected bird-name display options, the 10 most recent sounds
played, and custom species list selections. These data remain on the device
itself and are not sent to app-operated servers in this version.

In this version, we do not require login, we do not intentionally collect
in-app analytics or personal profile data, and we do not share user profile
information from inside the app. If the app is installed, updated, or later
purchased through Google Play, Google may process technical information under
its own policies and terms.

The main guide is intended for offline use. The `Ouvir mais (online)` item is
currently only a placeholder for a future feature and does not provide
streaming in this version. This version also does not request microphone,
contacts, photos, or external storage permissions for normal app use.

These local data remain on the device until the user clears the app's data or
uninstalls the app.

If future versions add online features, in-app purchases, downloadable asset
packs, analytics, or other data-processing behavior, this policy must be
updated before publication.
